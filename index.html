How to Use This Tool

  1. Select your Date of Birth in the first field.
  2. Choose the Age at Date (defaults to today) to calculate your age on a specific date.
  3. Click Calculate Age to see your exact age in years, months, weeks, and days.
  4. Scroll down to see your next birthday countdown, zodiac sign, and the day of the week you were born.
  5. Click Download PNG to save a shareable age card image.

FAQ

How is age calculated?

Age is calculated by comparing your date of birth with the target date. It accounts for leap years and varying month lengths to give you the exact age.

Does this account for leap years?

Yes! The calculator uses JavaScript's built-in Date engine, which correctly handles all leap years past and future.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All calculations happen in your browser — your birth date is never sent to any server or stored anywhere.
